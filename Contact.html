    Postdoc researcher with the Building City Dashboards project in the National Centre for Geocomputation at Maynooth University in Maynooth, Ireland. I am a spatial data scientist working with spatio-temporal data, urban environmental sensor analysis, and big data visualization. With the BCD project, I develop forecasting models and work with local authorities and national statistical institutes to make data accessible and useful for the public and urban decision makers.
